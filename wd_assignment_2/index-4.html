About Us

Sweet Life was founded in the year 2014 by two visionary entrepreneurs, Sam and Michell Thompson. Sweet Life had gained widespread recognition for its signature creations. From classic flavours like rich chocolate and creamy vanilla to innovative masterpieces like lavender honey and cinnamon swirl, each scoop at Sweet Life was an indulgent experience. They had always envisioned a location where people might meet to enjoy scrumptious frozen delicacies while enjoying life's sweet moments. Mrs. Mitchell and Mr. Sam set out to realise their idea with tenacity and a secret family recipe passed down through centuries.

Sorbet

Sorbet is a fruit-forward frozen treat with no dairy whatsoever. It includes only two ingredients: fruit puree and sugar.

Rolled Ice Cream

The basic components (milk, cream, sugar) are heated, aerated, then swiftly chilled to form Swiss roll-style ribbons of ice cream that may be served upright in a cup and topped with a variety of toppings.
