MY HOMETOWN:

I am from a small city called "Chau Doc". Chau Doc is a city located in the southwestern region of Vietnam, in An Giang province. My hometown is very interesting, it is also named as the land of the mountains amd rivers. The atmosphere in my hometown is very poetic and peaceful. Surrounded by mountains and trees, creating a fresh and cool atmosphere. My hometown is quite bustling because it stands out with a historical site that is Shrine of the Sam Mountain Goddess. "Shrine of the Sam Mountain Goddess" is very sacred and dignified. Every year, tourists visit and pray for blessings. In addition, my hometown is also famous for its rustic and delicious dished of the Southwest region of Vietnam. The people of my hometown are honest and naive.

My hometown is known for its river raft houses, because it is situated at the intersection of the Mekong and Bassac rivers, near the border with Cambodia. Therefore, we have a pretty close relationship with our neighbor Cambodia. Furthermore, my hometown also has immense green fields, there are blooming rice and farmers are cultivating. It is such a peaceful and precious scene in my life. I really love my hometown, because it is also where I was born and raised. I know that I will always remember it. No matter where I go, I promise that I always have a small place for my hometown in my heart.
